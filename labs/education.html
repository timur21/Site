Training 2012-2013 Year

  • Web Development Course


    New SELF-STUDIED Course for Web Development is going to be held in Lab 4 on Mondays at 12:30.

    Topic: Ruby on Rails 3 Framework.
    Course Starts on 22.04.2013
    The course will end at the end of May 2013

    Goals:
    1. To get acquainted with Ruby Programming Language.
    2. To learn Rails Framework (how to develop rails application)
    3. To learn how to upload and deploy Rails application on Server
    4. TDD (Test Driven Development)
    5. BDD (Behavior Driven Development) (optional)

    Prerequisites:
    Know at least one OOP Language (C++, Java, Python (preferable))
    Ebooks: Will be given
    Video Lectures: Will be given
    Practice in Lab 4.
    Project: CMS (Content Management System) web site will be produced.
    If you are very interested in web development we will be waiting for you.


  • The Research & Development (R & D) Club


    Research & Development (R & D) Club is established with a mission to advance science and technology, conduct research and support student researchers of IAAU. The R & D Club is going to be responsible for promoting, developing, organizing, conducting and coordinating research and development in line with university's targets and priorities. Our vision is to be an innovative, guiding, participating and cooperating club in the fields of science and technology, which serves for improvement of the life standards of our society and sustainable development of our university.
